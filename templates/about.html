EchriDar

Buying real estates can be hard and challenging in most cases. Buyers are assigned to do a lot of research to find the most appropriate and cost-effective real estate. For that reason, the main idea of EchriDar is to make this task easier. This project is developped by a group of engineering students who used machine learning to predict real estates prices.
